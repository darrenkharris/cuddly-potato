Privacy

A straightforward overview of how this website handles information.

Last updated: June 2026

This is a static personal website for Darren Harris, based at darren-harris.co.uk. It does not run a database, does not have a backend server, and does not operate a membership or accounts system.

Please note: This page is provided for transparency and is not intended as formal legal advice. If you have specific concerns about your data rights, you may wish to seek independent guidance.

What information is collected?

This website does not actively collect personal information from visitors. There is no contact form, no newsletter sign-up and no login functionality on this site.

If you choose to contact me directly — by clicking a linked platform such as LinkedIn or by emailing the address listed on this site, any information you provide as part of that communication will be handled solely in the context of responding to you. I do not share that information with third parties.

Cookies and tracking

This site does not set its own cookies and does not use analytics or tracking software by default.

Web hosting platforms (such as Cloudflare Pages, Netlify or GitHub Pages) may collect basic server log data, such as IP addresses and browser types, as part of normal infrastructure operation. This is governed by their own privacy policies, not mine.

External platforms

This website contains links to external platforms where I publish content, share resources and connect professionally. Each of these platforms operates under its own terms and privacy policy:

  • LinkedIn — linkedin.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Gumroad — gumroad.com/privacy
  • Amazon — Amazon UK Privacy Notice
  • Mixcloud — mixcloud.com/privacy
  • Instagram — Instagram Privacy Policy

When you click through to any of these platforms, their terms and policies apply. I encourage you to review them if you have questions about how those services handle your data.

Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you have rights regarding personal information held about you. Because this website does not collect or store personal data, most of these rights are unlikely to apply here in practice. However, if you believe I hold any information about you and would like to raise a concern, you are welcome to contact me directly via the email address on the Contact section.

Changes to this page

If this website changes in a way that affects how information is handled, this page will be updated accordingly. The date at the top of the page will reflect when it was last revised.

Questions

If you have any questions about this privacy information, please get in touch via the Contact section of this site.
