Practical, no-jargon guides for school and Multi Academy Trust IT leaders — covering the full breadth of digital technology leadership in education.

About the Guides

Written by a practitioner, for practitioners.

These guides are grounded in nearly 30 years of real-world experience in education technology leadership. They are written for IT directors, network managers, business managers, headteachers and trust leaders who need clear, actionable frameworks — not academic theory.

Each guide can be used as a standalone resource, a starting point for policy development, or a professional development tool for IT teams and senior leaders. Available on Gumroad as digital download.
