Background

Almost three decades in the room where technology meets education.

I am Darren Harris — an IT and digital technology leader whose career has taken me from hands-on technical support and network engineering, through infrastructure management, and on to senior strategic leadership roles across UK and international education settings.

My experience spans local authority maintained schools, independent education providers, national education organisations, and Multi Academy Trusts of varying sizes and complexities. That breadth gives me a perspective that is difficult to gain in any single role: I understand the pressures facing governors, headteachers and business leaders, the expectations of technical teams, and the day-to-day realities of the users who depend on technology to learn and teach.

Today, my focus is on practical, strategic guidance — helping schools and MATs build digital environments that are secure, well-governed, future-ready, and genuinely useful to the people within them.

Life & Perspective

Travel, experience and a wider view of the world.

A career spanning national and international education environments has taken me to many places, and that experience has shaped how I think and work. Exposure to different cultures, education systems and ways of solving problems has made me a better leader, a more lateral thinker, and a more empathetic colleague.

Travel is not just a pastime — it is part of how I stay curious. Whether it is exploring a new city, experiencing a different culture or simply finding space to think away from the day-to-day, those experiences feed directly back into the quality of the work I do.

That global perspective is something I try to bring into the guidance and resources I create — contextual, considered, and aware that there is rarely just one way to solve a problem.
